{ Help Teenagers to identify cyberbullying and seek professional helps }

According to Online Harm White Paper 2019, 20% of teenagers reported having cyberbullying. More importantly, around 30% of teenagers who were cyberbullied wanted to seek revenge. 

We need to stop this toxic loop. The conversation design of this chatbot focuses on one group of stakeholder - teenage girls who may have faced cyberbullying. In order to provide a more helpful and warm-hearted tone of voice, emojis and GIFs are also used.
