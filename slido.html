SLIDO

Transformable Design for Kindergarten Entrance
PreviousNext

Rethink a Moment to a Journey

Transformable Design is not widely used in the field of product design because of its interdependency design and engineering. Door as an everyday product, how will transformable design challenge the perception and interaction experience of doors?

Transforming by rotation, Slido is transformed from a door to staircases and slide, with familiar interface and experience for children. The door transforms to become the part of the journey for them to go through.
